about

The project is intended to help Sanskrit learners benefit from the amara kosha. English translations, word lists and gender hints are provided.

Methodology:

  • The data of the amara kosha proper is taken from SanskritDocuments.org and marked with word boundaries
  • The word lists and gender information are taken from JNU. Variants fround in Haragovinda Shastry's Maniprabha commentary are added.
  • Entries are numbered to match the popular Nirnaya Sagar reprints of the amara kosha
  • English translations are added, based on the Colebrooke edition of the amara kosha and Apte's dictionary
  • The Sanskrit vrittis are added (mostly identical to the vakya sudha commentary)
